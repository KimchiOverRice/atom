IMPACT OF COVID-19 ON AVIATION IN AMERICA

The 2020 coronavirus pandemic has impacted all aspects of life in America, as millions who have been hospitalized with COVID-19 and others partake in social distancing in fear of contracting the virus. This fear has carried over to the aviation industry, many aspects of which have been severely impacted by this outbreak.


IMPACTED ASPECTS OF THE AIR INDUSTRY:

Passenger Air Travel - this section goes over the changes that air passenger travel has gone through. This includes the change in air passenger numbers and passenger experience.

Economics - this section is on the financial impact of COVID-19 on airlines. It goes over company losses and government funding.

Environment - this section goes over the environmental impact of aircraft and covers how COVID-19 has altered these impacts.

Manufacturing - this section goes over the impact of COVID-19 on aircraft manufacturing. The section focuses on the mnaufacturing company Boeing.

Cargo - this section is on the impact of COVID-19 on air cargo. It goes over general industry drops in activity along with an analysis on specific companies.
